# 🗄️ Customer Behavior Analysis (SQL)

## 📌 Overview
This project uses SQL to analyze customer and sales data in order to extract meaningful insights about business performance and customer behavior.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify top customers and their purchasing patterns
- Analyze revenue by country or region
- Detect inactive customers (churn)
- Understand product performance

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- SQL (PostgreSQL / MySQL)
- Optional: Python for visualization
## 📁 Dataset
- E-commerce dataset / Customer dataset

## 🔍 Key Queries
- Top customers by total spending
- Revenue by country
- Monthly sales trends
- Customers with no recent activity (churn detection)
- Best-selling products

## 📊 Example Insights
- A small percentage of customers generate most of the revenue
- Certain regions show higher engagement and sales
- Identified a segment of inactive customers at risk of churn

## 💡 Business Impact
- Helps improve customer retention strategies
- Supports targeted marketing campaigns
- Optimizes sales and product strategies
